The Panchakarma Experience

Our signature 3-phase detoxification program is customized to your unique constitution and health needs:

Preparation Phase (Purvakarma)

Your body is prepared for deep cleansing through oleation (Snehana) and fomentation (Swedana) therapies.

Main Procedures (Pradhanakarma)

The five cleansing actions are administered based on your needs: Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana.

Rejuvenation Phase (Paschatkarma)

Specialized diet, herbs, and lifestyle practices to rebuild your system stronger than before.

Ayurvedic Wisdom

Health Tips & Guidance

Practical Ayurvedic advice for common health concerns based on your unique constitution.

Managing Diabetes Naturally

Blood Sugar Balance

Key Recommendations

  • Bitter is better:Include bitter gourd, fenugreek, neem, and turmeric in your diet.
  • Timing matters:Eat your largest meal at lunch when digestive fire is strongest.
  • Movement:Walk for 30 minutes after meals to improve glucose metabolism.
  • Herbal support:Try our Madhuhara tea blend with jamun, gurmar, and daruharidra.

Holistic Approach to PCOS

Hormonal Balance

Key Recommendations

  • Seed Cycling:Incorporate flax, pumpkin, and sesame seeds to support hormonal phases.
  • Stress Reduction:Practice yoga and meditation to lower cortisol levels.
  • Avoid Processed Foods:Focus on whole, unprocessed foods to reduce inflammation.
  • Herbal Allies:Consider herbs like Shatavari and Ashwagandha under expert guidance.

Ayurvedic Weight Management

Healthy Metabolism

Key Recommendations

  • Ignite Agni:Start your day with warm ginger-lemon water to boost digestive fire.
  • Eat Mindfully:Eat according to your Dosha, focusing on warm, light, and spiced foods.
  • Active Lifestyle:Incorporate dynamic yoga or brisk walking into your daily routine.
  • Therapeutic Support:Therapies like Udvartana (herbal powder massage) can aid metabolism.

Managing Flu & Cold

Immunity Boost

Key Recommendations

  • Herbal Brews:Sip on warm teas made with Tulsi, Ginger, and Black Pepper throughout the day.
  • Light Diet:Consume warm, light foods like vegetable soup and khichdi.
  • Steam Inhalation:Use eucalyptus or mint oil in hot water to clear congestion.
  • Ample Rest:Allow your body to heal by getting plenty of rest and sleep.

Daily Routines (Dinacharya)

Align your daily activities with nature's rhythms for optimal health and vitality.

Routine for Children

Establishing a consistent routine for children is crucial for their physical growth, mental stability, and building healthy lifelong habits.

Morning (6:30-8 AM)

Rise & Shine

  • Wake up with the sun to feel fresh and energetic.
  • Drink a glass of warm water.
  • Engage in light stretching or a few rounds of Sun Salutation.
Midday (12-1 PM)

Nourish & Grow

  • Have the largest meal of the day, rich in all nutrients.
  • Focus on whole foods and avoid processed snacks.
  • Encourage mindful eating without screen time.
Evening (7-9 PM)

Wind Down

  • A light, easily digestible dinner at least 2 hours before bed.
  • Engage in calming activities like reading.
  • A warm milk with turmeric can promote restful sleep.
